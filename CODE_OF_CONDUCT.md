# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on technical content, not people.
- Accept feedback and discuss disagreements in good faith.
- Help keep issues and pull requests actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Doxxing or sharing private/sensitive information
- Spam or disruptive conduct

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.

For concerns, contact: admin@exahia.ia
